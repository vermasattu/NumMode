NumMode

Convert your keyboard into a functional numpad with a single toggle!

Features

  • Toggle Shortcut: Press Ctrl + ` to switch between normal typing and numpad mode.
  • Tray Menu Control: Right-click the tray icon → Toggle Numpad or Exit.
  • Visual Status: Tray tooltip shows whether numpad mode is ✅ ON or ❌ OFF.
  • True Numpad Mode: Only assigned keys work as numpad buttons; all other letters are blocked.
  • Mapped Keys:
    • U I O → 7 8 9
    • J K L → 4 5 6
    • M , . → 1 2 3
    • Space → 0
    • Enter → Numpad Enter
    • ; → +
    • / → −
    • P → *
    • [ → ÷
  • Portable EXE: Runs without installation.

Who It’s For

  • Users who don’t have a physical numpad.
  • Data entry operators, accountants, spreadsheet users.
  • Gamers who want a temporary numpad layout.

How To Use

  1. Run NumMode.exe → tray icon appears.
  2. Press Ctrl + ` to activate numpad mode (ON ✅).
  3. Press again to turn off (OFF ❌).
  4. Exit anytime from the tray menu.

Auto-Launch at Windows Startup

  1. Press Win + R and type shell:startup, then press Enter.
  2. Copy NumMode.exe (or a shortcut) into the Startup folder.
  3. NumMode will launch automatically on PC startup.
